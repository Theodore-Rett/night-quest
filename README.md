# Night Quest
Night Quest is a 2D top down adventure game. Inspired by early Zelda games, and other early dungeon crawl games. Following the story of a young farmer, who dreams of becoming a knight. He sneaks out at night to begin his journey north to the capitol city. Hoping to join the lists at the summer tourney, and prove himself worthy of becoming a true knight. Along his adventure he slowly uncovers the plot of an evil wizard, attempting the take power by plaguing the kingdom in shadow creatures every night. And we follow him on his journey to bring peace to the realm.

## Project Tools
- Godot 4.5
- GDScript
- Aseprite

## Instillation Instructions 
1. Create a local git clone of the project from the repository. [Found Here](https://github.com/Theodore-Rett/night-quest)
2. Open project folder in Godot v.4.5

## Known Bugs
- **Stuck Between Wall and Enemy**: At some angles the player can get "pinned" to a wall by an enemy and are unable to move away.
- **Player Stuck in Place**: The run animation will play on loop, but the player is stuck in a singular location and can take no actions. Cause unknown, but has happened on multiple occasions.
